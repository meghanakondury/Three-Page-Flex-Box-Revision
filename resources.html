Want to learn more about gender stereotypes?

Here are some additional resources, articles and images to learn more about the unconscious gender stereotypes and biases around us.

'The Brilliance Trap' by Andrei Cimpian and Sarah-Jane Leslie is a great article that explains how misplaced emphasis on genius subtly discourages women and African-Americans from entering certain academic fields'

“Is this Family Gender-Biased?” by KJ Dell’Antonia highlights how everything around us is based on stereotypical notions

"Are Girls Bad at Chess?" by Daisy Grewal
